Falaknuma Palace

Overview

Falaknuma Palace meaning “Mirror of the Sky”. Falaknuma Palace Hyderabad stands majestically on a 2000-foot-high hill, spreading across 32 acres.It was built by Nawab Vikar-ul-Umra. This palace is of Indo-Islamic and European architectural styles with beautifully landscaped gardens.

This Palace was taken over by Taj Group and has been opened as 5-star hotel in the year of 2010. Now known as the Taj Falaknuma Palace. The hotel is consisted of 60 spacious guestrooms offering all the visitors with a royal experience.

Visiting Falaknuma Palace is stepping back in time to experience the lavish lifestyle of the Nizams and historical treasure that offers a unique glimpse into Hyderabad's royal past.

  • Location : Engine Bowli, Falaknuma, Hyderabad.
  • Entry Fee : upto INR 3000/- per person.
