GOLCONDA FORT

Overview

Golconda Fort, initially constructed by Kakatiya Kings to defend the western part of their kingdom and completed by Rani Rudrama and her successor Prataparudra. The structure that we see today was renovated by the Qutb kings, who chose Golconda to be the center of his governance.

It stands as a remarkable engineering feat atop a granite hill. With eight gateways, numerous temples, mosques, stables, royal chambers, and ingenious moveable bridges.

Offering a compelling blend of architectural grandeur and historical importance. Perched high above the city, it remains a must-visit destination for those seeking to explore its magnificent past.

  • Location : Khair Complex, Ibrahim Bagh, Hyderabad.
  • Entry Fee : INR 15/- per person.
