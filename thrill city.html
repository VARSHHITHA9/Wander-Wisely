THRILL CITY

Overview

India's first futuristic park, It is a 2-acre park with more than 50 games and attractions, including thrill rides, virtual reality experiences, arcade games, and a splash water ride.

Thrill City is a popular destination for people of all ages, from children to adults to senior citizens. There is something for everyone at the park, whether you are looking for an adrenaline rush or a more relaxed experience.

It also has a food court where you can enjoy a variety of snacks and meals. There is also a gift shop where you can purchase souvenirs to remember your visit.

  • Operational Timings : 11:00 AM - 11:00 PM
  • Activity Duration : 6 hrs (approx)
  • Entry Fee : INR 1000/- per person (approx)
