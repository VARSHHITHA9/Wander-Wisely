Wonderla

Overview

It is the third Wonderla amusement park in India, after Bangalore and Kochi. The park is spread over 50 acres of land and offers a variety of rides and attractions. Also has a number of other attractions, including a 4D theater, a video arcade, and a bowling alley.

Wonderla offers chain of thrilling and entertaining experience for visitors of all ages. It also has a food court where you can enjoy a variety of snacks and meals. There is also a gift shop where you can purchase souvenirs to remember your visit.

  • Operational Timings : 11:00 AM - 06:00 PM
  • Activity Duration : 6 hrs (approx)
  • Entry Fee : INR 1300/- per person (approx)
